Camp session - Digital Humanities
------

Hamilton College is working with Discovery Garden on a specific Digital Humanities "sprout"

What does the group want in a Digital Humanities "sprout"
	* Working with book ingest pack:
		* don't like working with uncompressed tiff
		* need a range of variablity
		* an image that is scanned horizontally, which is then rotated vertically is incompatable
	* Desire for a process that can be interrupted or resumed
	* Image correction
	* want a streamlined process to update datastreams, and feedback to from process to say what is wrong...
		* low contrast
		* bad OCR
